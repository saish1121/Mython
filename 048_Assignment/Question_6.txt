6. In a classification report, explain the meaning of the following metrics:
Precision
Recall
F1 Score
Support
Accuracy

-> 
1) Precision means out of all the output predicted as positive how many of them where truely positive.
2) Recall means out of all positive how many were correctly predicted by the model
3) F1 Score gives the balance between Precision and Recall.
4) It gives the count of classes samples present in the dataset 
5) Accuracy means out of all predicted output how many were correct
