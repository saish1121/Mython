9. Why R2 cannot be greater than 1?
-> The formula of r2 is 

    r2 = summation of square of difference between predicted output and y mean / summation of square of difference between actual y and mean y 

    when the model is completely perfect then the 
    y_pred == y_test 

    therefore ,

    numerator == denominator 

    hence r2 cannot be greater than 1 cause 10/10 = 1 or value / value = 1
